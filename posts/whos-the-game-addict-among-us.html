Who's the game addict among us?

Among Us became more popular than a global pandemic in 2020 and I have a theory about why. It had nothing to do with spaceships and everything to do with loneliness.

Nakshatra RP · Feb 12, 2021 · 2 min read

There came a time in 2020 when the game Among Us became more popular than the global pandemic COVID-19. I barely used to play video games and whenever I did, there would be three levels of interest; Obsession Level, Playing it for-the-sake-of-playing Level and finally Uninstall-the-game Level. This wasn't the case with Among Us.

"Join your crewmates in a multiplayer game of teamwork and betrayal! Play online or over local WiFi with 4-10 players as you attempt to hold your spaceship together and return back to civilization. But beware...as there may be an alien impostor aboard!"

The first time I ever saw this game was on my friend's Instagram story last September but it seemed low-key dull to me. Few days later, I started seeing memes about it everywhere. At that time, my only friend who played Among Us explained to me about the game. I installed it and it was indeed cool! As I've mentioned, I wasn't a huge fan of games so I uninstalled it after three days (same story). That's when many of my friends started playing. It became the #1 trend which spread like wildfire. I installed it again and I slowly got obsessed with the game. I've never lost interest ever since.

But why did Among Us skyrocket in popularity in 2020 despite its introduction in 2018? Some of my friends got really addicted to it and spent hours on the game. The first reason is, of course, the influencers and streamers who brought this game to the limelight. But why wasn't it like the other games? The home quarantine, due to the pandemic, was the time when people craved social interaction. Even introverts (like me) wished to spend time with friends. Among Us became a platform to socialize. For some people, it meant meeting new people and for others, hanging out with friends virtually. The ease of playing it also allowed every newbie to catch up quickly.

Currently, fans are eagerly waiting for the release of a new map "The Airship". The laughs and lies to be continued...
