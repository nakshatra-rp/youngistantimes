"Are you the God of Hammers?": When Marvel taught us life lessons

Thor losing his hammer, Carol Danvers breaking free. Turns out Marvel has been quietly teaching us things about potential and identity that no motivational poster ever managed to.

Nakshatra RP · Feb 15, 2021 · 2 min read

There can be times when we feel hopeless thinking about what we don't have. I come from a middle class family and whenever I used to see wealthier people, I used to feel that they have a higher chance of success because they can avail more resources compared to me. My perspective about success has changed over the course of years mainly due to influential figures, including fictional characters.

In the 2017 Marvel Studios superhero film "Thor: Ragnarok", there comes a situation when the Norse God of thunder questions his ability to defeat his foe after his hammer was destroyed. He says to his father, "Without my hammer, I can't." Odin replies, "Are you Thor, the God of Hammers? That hammer was to help you control your power; to focus it. It was never your source of strength." These words had a deep meaning which weren't merely about a God and his hammer. It is a universal truth that applies to every forms of life that exist. Resources are something which helps you bring out the potential within yourself. They're not the source of the potential itself. Eventually in the movie, Thor unleashes his powers to defeat the villain without a weapon. This taught me that wealth and material possessions don't determine my strength and success.

"I've been fighting with one arm tied behind my back. But what happens when I'm finally set free?", is another iconic line from the 2019 "Captain Marvel". This refers to how the protagonist, Carol Danvers discovers her full potential after being held back. Seeing this quote from a personal perspective, it means that we shouldn't let our own mindset hold us back. Don't let your background interfere in your success. Realize your potential and work hard to overcome your limitations.

Fiction is mainly created to entertain the audience but it can also be a source of inspiration if you try to interpret the situations and quotes. Marvel Studios continues to entertain the fandom with new projects and inspire us through its characters.
